# Shopping Behavior Insights Dashboard

## Project Overview
This repository contains a **Power BI Dashboard** focused on analyzing consumer purchasing behavior. By evaluating demographic distributions, regional sales, product preferences, and shipping choices, this project transforms raw retail data into actionable business intelligence. The goal is to help stakeholders understand what drives customer behavior and where revenue opportunities lie.

## Business Objective
The primary objective of this project is to optimize retail sales strategies and enhance customer retention by:
*   Identifying high-performing product categories and item types.
*   Understanding the demographic profile (age and gender) of the core customer base.
*   Analyzing purchasing frequency and preferences to improve targeted marketing campaigns.
*   Evaluating fulfillment methods (shipping types) to optimize logistics and customer satisfaction.
*   **Pinpointing high-value geographical markets to optimize regional resource allocation.**

## Tools Used
*   **Data Visualization:** Power BI Desktop
*   **Data Transformation:** Power Query (ETL process)

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The dashboard tracks four primary high-level metrics to gauge business health at a glance:
*   **Total Revenue:** \$233K
*   **Average Order Value (AOV):** \$60
*   **Total Customers:** 3,900
*   **Average Review Rating:** 3.75 / 5.0

## Dashboard Features & Visualizations
*   **Demographic Segmentation:** A grouped bar chart tracking *Age Group and Gender Distribution* across Adult, Old, and Young categories.
*   **Purchase Frequency Analysis:** A horizontal bar chart identifying how often customers return to buy (e.g., Every 3 Months, Annually, Monthly, etc.).
*   **Seasonality Insights:** Tracking customer counts or sales volumes by *Shipping Type/Season* (Spring, Fall, Winter, Summer).
*   **Product Performance:** 
    *   *Revenue by Category:* Vertical bar chart showcasing top earners (Clothing, Accessories, Footwear, Outerwear).
    *   *Top 10 Purchase Items by Revenue:* Ranked list highlighting specific top sellers like Blouses, Shirts, and Dresses.
*   **Customer Preferences:** A breakdown of the *Top 10 Preferred Colors* by customers (led by Olive and Yellow).
*   **Fulfillment Breakdown:** A donut chart displaying *Customer Distribution by Shipping Type* (Free Shipping vs. Standard vs. Store Pickup).
*   **Geographical Analysis:** 
    *   *Geographical Sales Distribution:* A filled US map visual showing geographic density.
    *   *Total Revenue by Location:* **A detailed breakdown bar chart ranking revenue across states, explicitly highlighting top contributors like Montana, Illinois, California, Idaho, and Nevada.**
*   **Interactive Slicers:** Dynamic filtering capabilities based on **Customer Satisfaction** and **Payment Method**.

## Key Insights
*   **Demographic Dominance:** The "Adult" age group represents the largest customer segment, with male consumers showing strong representation across multiple age brackets.
*   **Core Categories:** **Clothing** is the clear primary driver of revenue, followed by Accessories. Drill-down data shows **Blouses** and **Shirts** leading individual item revenue.
*   **Even Shipping Splits:** Customer preference is almost perfectly split across fulfillment methods, with Free Shipping (34.11%), Standard Shipping (33.05%), and Store Pickup (32.84%) holding nearly equal shares.
*   **Top Performing States:** **Montana, Illinois, California, Idaho, and Nevada** emerge as the leading states generating the highest total revenue, closely clustered near the top of the location performance bracket.
*   **Room for Improvement:** An average review rating of **3.75** suggests that while sales are steady, there are underlying friction points in product quality or customer service that need addressing.

## Recommendations
1.  **Targeted Marketing:** Design specific marketing campaigns tailored to the "Adult" demographic, specifically highlighting the top-selling items like blouses and shirts.
2.  **Geographical Expansion:** Allocate a higher percentage of the digital marketing budget to top-performing states (**Montana, Illinois, and California**) to maximize regional ROI.
3.  **Improve Customer Satisfaction:** Investigate the drivers behind the 3.75 average rating. Use the "Customer Satisfaction" slicer to isolate low-rating transactions and identify if specific items or shipping delays are causing dissatisfaction in lower-performing states.
4.  **Capitalize on Color Preferences:** Ensure inventory levels for top-preferred colors (like Olive, Yellow, and Silver) are well-stocked, especially during peak purchasing seasons.
5.  **Leverage Store Pickup:** Since nearly 33% of customers utilize Store Pickup, implement "buy-online-pickup-in-store" (BOPIS) exclusive offers to drive foot traffic and potential impulse buys at brick-and-mortar locations.

## Conclusion
The **Shopping Behavior Insights** dashboard successfully highlights the strong areas of the retail business—namely a robust adult customer base, highly balanced delivery ecosystems, and reliable anchor states like Montana and Illinois driving revenue. By addressing the mid-tier review ratings and leaning heavily into high-revenue clothing categories in top performing states, the business is well-positioned to scale its total revenue beyond the current \$233K mark.
